Our Mission

"To make Japanese language learning accessible, efficient, and enjoyable for everyone through innovative technology and proven learning methods."

Shutokun was born from a passion for Japanese language and culture, combined with a desire to create a better learning experience. We believe that learning Japanese should be engaging, effective, and tailored to each individual's needs.

What Makes Shutokun Different?

  • Comprehensive JLPT preparation from N5 to N1
  • Smart SRS system that adapts to your learning pace
  • High-quality example sentences and audio
  • Progress tracking across all devices
  • Free access to core features
  • Regular updates with new content
