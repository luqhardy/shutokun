Getting Started

Welcome to Shutokun! This guide will help you make the most of your Japanese learning journey.

  • Sign in with your Google account to save your progress
  • Choose your JLPT level (N5 to N1)
  • Select your study category (Vocabulary, Kanji, or Grammar)
  • Start learning with our SRS system

Study Modes

Hiragana/Katakana Practice

Perfect for beginners! Master the Japanese writing system with our interactive practice mode.

JLPT Levels

Choose from N5 (beginner) to N1 (advanced) to study vocabulary, kanji, and grammar specific to each level.

Free Mode

Mix and match content from different levels to create your custom study session.

SRS System

Our Spaced Repetition System helps you learn efficiently:

  • Review cards at optimal intervals
  • Track your progress with detailed statistics
  • Focus on difficult items with smart scheduling
  • Save your progress across devices

Tips for Success

  • Study regularly for best results
  • Use the SRS system consistently
  • Review difficult items more frequently
  • Take advantage of example sentences
  • Practice writing kanji and vocabulary
